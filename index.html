Lamborghini Huracan EVO Rear Wheel Drive (RWD)

PRICE: Rs 3.22 crore

  • Digital touch

    Inside the cockpit, there is an 8.4-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple Carplay. Also, it gets a fully digital instrument cluster.

  • Lamborghini Huracan EVO exterior

    On the outside, the Huracan gets Y-shaped electric concept headlamps, a new front bumper, wider air intakes that get vertical fins, a black rear bumper and F-duct spoiler.

  • Engine

    The Huracan Evo RWD is powered by a 5.2-litre naturally-aspirated V10 engine that can churn out 618 PS of peak power and 560 Nm of torque. Paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, the engine sends power to the rear wheels. Lamborghini claims the Huracan Evo RWD can run at a top speed of 325 kmph and it can accelerate 0-100 kmph in 3.3 seconds.

  • About Performance Traction Control System

    Huracan Evo RWD gets Performance Traction Control System (P-TCS) that assures better torque delivery in different conditions. There are as many as three driving modes -- Strada, Sport and Corsa.
